Installation Steps

1
Open the DMG & Drag to Applications

Double-click the downloaded .dmg file and drag the Daybar icon into the Applications folder alias.

2
Launch Daybar

Open Daybar from your Launchpad or Applications folder. Since it's a new download, macOS may ask you to confirm opening it safely.

3
Reclaim your menu bar

Click the Daybar icon in your macOS menu bar at the top right of your screen. Your 7-Day Pro Trial is automatically active!
